6.1 - Solving Linear Systems

When given two systems, we typically are required to find the intercept. In this unit, the two equations would be linear equations that have a degree of 1.



There are three types of methods to solve Linear systems:
1. Graphing Method
2. Addition Method
3. Substitution Method
Typically, pre-calculus courses would teach all three methods but ideally, the substitution method would be better as it is the fastest and the most accurate method with 0% chance of error unless you make an error yourself.
